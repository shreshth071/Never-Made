Policy

Architecting transparency. Understanding how your digital footprint is managed within the Never Made ecosystem.

01

Data Acquisition

When you establish an identity with Never Made, we acquire specific data points to facilitate your experience. This includes but is not limited to: your name, digital correspondence address, shipping coordinates, and acquisition history.

Technical telemetry such as IP addresses, browser variants, and interaction patterns are captured to optimize the digital interface performance.

02

Utilization Framework

Your data is utilized within a strictly defined framework:

  • Processing and delivering physical archives (orders).
  • Transmitting critical drop information and exclusive access codes.
  • Enhancing the Cyber-Industrial UI/UX of our digital platforms.
  • Preventing fraudulent interactions and maintaining system integrity.
03

External Protocols

We do not liquidate your data to external entities. However, data is shared with essential protocol partners (payment gateways, logistics units) purely for operational completion.

All external partners are required to maintain strict encryption standards consistent with our internal security architecture.

04

Security Architecture

We implement heavy-weight encryption and multi-layer firewall protocols to protect your identity. While no digital transmission is 100% impervious, our architecture is designed to exceed industry safety standards.
